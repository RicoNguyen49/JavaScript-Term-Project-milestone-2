Game: Tic-Tac-Toe

Description:
Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid with their respective symbols. 
The goal is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols.


Game: Space Exploration

Description:
Space exploration takes inspiration  from flappy bird the classic space invaders arcade  game but without the invaders. The goal of the game is to achieve  the highest score, traveling through different levels as it gets faster and obstacles  change at each milestone.
The user will control the spaceship with the arrow keys and have to advoid hitting the lazer obstacles as it traverse the universe. 
The lazers will change colors randomly with most of them being bright and easy to see. the game includes a black out stage where the lazers will turn black making it harder for the user to advoid the obstacle. 


Game: Retro Match

Description:
For this game i will be creating a card matching game. the goal is to selection a retro logo or game and trying to match it with the correct name for that logo/game. 
currently in development.
